## Getting there
- Closest airport: Charolette, Richmond, or Raleigh are the three major options.
- Drive time: 3-5 hours depending on airport.
- Trailhead / entry point: Grayson Highlands State Park

## Time & season
- Best months: Summer through the fall. May - Oct
- Typical start time: Flexible but park closes at 10PM.
- Permit / reservation: State Park entry required, 10$

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: The Kentucky and North Carolina highpoints are a few hours drive away each.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Can get quite windy, prone to storms but peak is well sheltered.
