## Getting there
- Closest airport: Tri-Cities (TRI) or Knoxville (TYS)
- Drive time: ~1–2 hrs from TRI / ~2.5–3.5 hrs from TYS (depending on route)
- Trailhead / entry point: Harlan County side via KY-160 toward the VA line; access is commonly via the ridge road network—expect rough/steep sections

## Time & season
- Best months: May–October (clearer roads), fall colors in October
- Typical start time: Morning—fog/low clouds are common
- Permit / reservation: None typically, but access can vary (roads/land ownership); check current conditions before going

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Breaks Interstate Park (VA/KY), Cumberland Gap area, Red River Gorge (longer detour)
- Good multi-day combo: Chain with nearby Appalachian highpoints (VA/WV/PA) on an East Coast highpoint run

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Sturdy shoes; road can be muddy/washed out—consider high-clearance vehicle
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Fog, sudden storms, icy roads in winter/early spring
