## Getting there
- Closest airport: Denver
- Drive time: 2 Hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Bear Lake trailhead is the most popular trailhead.

## Time & season
- Best months: Summer - Fall for weather and access. May - Oct
- Typical start time: Flexible
- Permit / reservation: Standard national park pass

## What to pair it with
- Good multi-day combo: You'll probably be spending some time staying nearby in Denver or Fort Collins.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Summer storms are common and can onset quickly. Be prepared to drop below treeline if they appear.
