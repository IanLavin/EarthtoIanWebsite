## Getting there
- Closest airport: Cusco
- Drive time: 3-4 hours from Cusco
- Trailhead / entry point: Almost all hikes start from Challacancha

## Time & season
- Best months: Peru dry season May - Sept
- Typical start time: Most tours depart Cusco early 4-5am
- Permit / reservation: None but often booked through tour companies

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Humantay lake is the most popular day trip sight to see.
- Good multi-day combo: For most people this will be expereinced as part of a multi day trek to Machu Picchu.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear, backpacking supplies if needed
- Water: Bring/buy your own or risk filtering
- Weather hazards: Due to the high altitude storms can occur year round. It also can get quite cold during the dry season, into the single digits.
