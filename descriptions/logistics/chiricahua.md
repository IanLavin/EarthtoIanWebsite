## Getting there
- Closest airport: Phoenix is the biggest one but Tucson can work as well.
- Drive time: 4 hours from Phoenix, 2 from Tucson.
- Trailhead / entry point: Almost all major hikes are located off of Bonita Canyon Drive.

## Time & season
- Best months: Spring - Fall provides best weather and temps. Due to the elevation can actually expereince snowfall during winter.
- Typical start time: Flexible
- Permit / reservation: Standard national park pass will grant entrance.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Tombstone is a relativly close Wild West ghost town that is one of the most famous in the West.
- Good multi-day combo: You will probably be combining this with a visit through Tucson to hit Saguaro National Park.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear.
- Water: Bring more than you think you will need.
- Weather hazards: Can be prone to summer monsoon storms and lightning as a result of its elevation.
