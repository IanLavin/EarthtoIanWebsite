## Getting there
- Closest airport: Calgary
- Drive time: 1:30 - 3 hours depending on traffic
- Trailhead / entry point: Some iconic locations include Lake Louise and Moraine Lake

## Time & season
- Best months:  July - Sept
- Typical start time: Flexible but early if hitting one of the popular desitnations like Lake Louise
- Permit / reservation: Parks Canada pass typically required

## What to pair it with
- Good multi-day combo: If time permits both Yoho National Park and Jasper National park are each a few hours away and breaktaking in their own rights.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: The rockies are prone to summer storms. During the late summer wildfires and smoke become a real issue as well
