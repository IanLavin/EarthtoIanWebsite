## Getting there
- Closest airport: St Louis
- Drive time: 2 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Taum Sauk Mountain State Park

## Time & season
- Best months: Spring–fall; fall foliage is a highlight
- Typical start time: Flexible
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Good multi-day combo: Mammoth Cave and Gateway arch are a few hours drive away.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear
- Water: Optional for short walks, bring more if hiking
- Weather hazards: Can be prone to storms in spring and summer
