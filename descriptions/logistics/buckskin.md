## Getting there
- Closest airport: Las Vegas is the closest major airport.
- Drive time: 4 hours from Vegas.
- Trailhead / entry point: Technically you can start from either end but most everyone will start at wire pass trailhead.

## Time & season
- Best months: Winter months are cold and often the canyon is flooded. Spring is hit or miss on flooding but ideal temps. Summer is hot and canyon is succeptable to flash floods. Fall is the best time overall.
- Typical start time: Early morning to beat the heat and the crowds.
- Permit / reservation: Permits are required to park at Wire pass trailhead. Overnight permits are also required for any camping and are quite difficult to get.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Very likely you will be pairing this will the Wave, Grand Staircase Escalante, or Page.
- Good multi-day combo: Southwest roadtrips are almost always the best way to experience this region. Many options for nearby sights.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear.
- Water: Bring more than you think you will need.
- Weather hazards: Canyon is extremly prone to flash flooding. Due to its depth and length you have almost no options for a quick exit once inside. Be aware of the weather forecast and play it safe if rain is forecast.
