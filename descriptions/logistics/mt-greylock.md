## Getting there
- Closest airport: Albany (ALB)
- Drive time: ~1–1.5 hrs from ALB
- Trailhead / entry point: Mt. Greylock State Reservation; summit access depends on season (road closures). Hike options from multiple trailheads

## Time & season
- Best months: Late spring–fall; fall foliage is top-tier
- Typical start time: Early morning for clear views
- Permit / reservation: No permit; seasonal road access varies

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Berkshires towns (Williamstown, North Adams), MASS MoCA
- Good multi-day combo: MA + CT (Frissell) + RI (Jerimoth) / VT/NH if extending

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Layers—summit is cooler/windier; good hiking shoes if road is closed
- Water: Bring at least 1L (more if hiking up)
- Weather hazards: Rapid weather shifts; snow/ice lingering at elevation
