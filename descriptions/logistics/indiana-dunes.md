## Getting there
- Closest airport: Chicago ORD or MDW
- Drive time: 45 mins to 1 hour depending on traffic
- Trailhead / entry point: Three dunes trailhead

## Time & season
- Best months: June - Oct
- Typical start time: Daylight hours
- Permit / reservation: Standard national park pass

## What to pair it with
- Good multi-day combo: Usually best combined with exploring the city of Chicago itself

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Can be prone to snow and wind during winter
