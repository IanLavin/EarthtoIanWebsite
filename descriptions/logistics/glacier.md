## Getting there
- Closest airport: Kalispell 
- Drive time: 1 hour
- Trailhead / entry point: Logan Pass, Many Glacier, and St Marys are some of the most popular areas.

## Time & season
- Best months: Summer June - Sept

## What to pair it with
- Good multi-day combo: Due to its remote location it usually requires a dedicated trip, but could still be combined with Yellowstone and Grand Teton if desired.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Regular hiking gear.
- Water: Bring your own.
- Weather hazards: Winter snow can be heavy and the going the sun road often wont fully open until early July. Also prone to afternoon storms in the summer.
