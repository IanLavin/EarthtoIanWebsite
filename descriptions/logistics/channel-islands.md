## Getting there
- Closest airport: Los Angeles
- Drive time: 1-2 hours driving, 1-2 hours by boat.
- Trailhead / entry point: Only accessible via boat tours from Oxnard.

## Time & season
- Best months: Spring is the best month as the islands are greenest but wind can be a concern. Summer and Fall are stable weather but often qutie hot.
- Typical start time: You will be working off of the boat schedule. Most depart early moring around 8AM and return 4-5PM.
- Permit / reservation: National park pass required, plus boat transportation.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: You'll probably be combing this with a trip to see Los Angeles.
- Good multi-day combo: If youre looking for a bit more adventure, Mount San Antonio provdes dramatic mountains while Highway 1 has some stunning beaches along it.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard gear if hiking, Kayak and lifejacket if exploring sea caves.
- Water: None on island so bring your own.
- Weather hazards: The islands are often quite windy and if wind speeds are high enough all boat tours are canceled for the day. Keep an eye on wind speed for your reserved day.
