## Getting there
- Closest airport: Las Vegas
- Drive time: 3-4 hours from Vegas, longer from SLC
- Trailhead / entry point: Numerous trails and viewpoitns.

## Time & season
- Best months: Shoulder months of spring and fall are the most plesant. March - May or Sept - Nov
- Typical start time: Early to avoid afternoon heat.
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: You'll almost certainly be combining this with some of the national parks it borders such as Capitol Reef or Bryce.
- Good multi-day combo: Best experienced as part of a road trip across the southwest.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear
- Water: Bring plenty
- Weather hazards: Sun exposure are the biggest concerns like most of the Southwest.
