## Getting there
- Closest airport: Denver or Albuquerque
- Drive time: 4 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: High Dune Loop Trailhead.

## Time & season
- Best months: Late spring for a chance at seeing Medano Creek. Summer is also decent. May - Aug
- Typical start time: Flexible
- Permit / reservation: Standard national park pass.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Zapata Falls or Blanca Peak.
- Good multi-day combo: Usually best done when combined with other nearby attractions such as Taos New Mexico or Rio Grande National Forest.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear, Skis/Sandboard if intending to ride down the dunes.
- Water: Bring your own.
- Weather hazards: Like most of Colorado prone to summer storms.
