## Getting there
- Closest airport: Portland is the biggest but Eugene or Salem can also work
- Drive time: 4-6 hours depending on airport.
- Trailhead / entry point: Popular hikes include tall trees grove, boy scout trail, and Lady Bird Johnson Grove.

## Time & season
- Best months: Summer as the park can be cold and wet especially during winter. May - Sept
- Typical start time: Early morning is best for parking.
- Permit / reservation: Standard national park pass.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Crater Lake national park is only a few hours away. Shasta is also a few hours drive away.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard Hiking Gear.
- Water: Bring your own.
- Weather hazards: Prone to large ammounts of rain during the winter months.
