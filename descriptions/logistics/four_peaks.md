## Getting there
- Closest airport: Phoenix
- Drive time: 2 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Lone Pine Trailhead

## Time & season
- Best months: Summer as the summit can have snow/ice in winter. May - Oct
- Typical start time: Early morning to avoid thunderstorms.
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: You will likely pass by Theodore Roosevelt Lake on your way out. It can be a nice stop.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear.
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Unlike most of Phoenix can be prone to snow and ice in winter. Also during monsoon season thunderstorms tend to congregate around the peak.
