## Getting there
- Closest airport: Salt Lake City.
- Drive time: 4 hours from SLC.
- Trailhead / entry point: Almost all major hikes branch off or connect to the rim trail.

## Time & season
- Best months: Can be visited year round but shoulder months provide best combo of clear weather and mild temps. April - May or Sept - Oct
- Typical start time: Early starts are prefereable to best the heat and the massive crowds.
- Permit / reservation: Standard national park pass.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Best combined with other nearby parks including Zion or Capitol Reef.
- Good multi-day combo: Very likely you will be doing a soutwest road trip hitting nearby national parks. But other close hidden gems include Lower Calf Creek or Coral Pink Sand Dunes.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear
- Water: Bring your own.
- Weather hazards: Extreme heat can be a problem during summer. Prepare for little shade and sun exposure.
