## Getting there
- Closest airport: Denver (DEN) (long drive) or regional airports in western KS/CO (limited)
- Drive time: Often approached as a road-trip stop; hours from major hubs
- Trailhead / entry point: Rural ranch area—follow signs to the parking/marker; short walk to the highpoint

## Time & season
- Best months: Spring and fall (less heat/wind)
- Typical start time: Morning or late afternoon (avoid peak heat)
- Permit / reservation: None; respect private land rules/signage

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Road-trip attractions in western KS / eastern CO; stargazing
- Good multi-day combo: Pair with Black Mesa (OK) + Panorama Point (NE) for a Plains highpoint run

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Sun protection is key; normal shoes
- Water: Bring water—services can be far away
- Weather hazards: Strong wind, thunderstorms, extreme summer heat
