### Getting There
Located in southern West Virginia near Fayetteville. Easily accessible by car.

### Time & Season
Spring through fall is best. Fall foliage is especially popular.

### Getting Around
Short hikes and scenic overlooks highlight the gorge. Some areas require driving between access points.

### What to Pair With
Pairs well with Appalachian road trips or other West Virginia outdoor areas.
