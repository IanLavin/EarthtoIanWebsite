## Getting there
- Closest airport: Washington DC is the closest major airport
- Drive time: 4-5 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Canyon rim visitor center.

## Time & season
- Best months: Late spring through summer for rafting or climbing May - Aug
- Typical start time: Flexible
- Permit / reservation: National Park pass

## What to pair it with
- Good multi-day combo: Can also be combined with other parks such as Shenandoah or highpoints such as Spruce Knob, Black Mountain, or Mt Rogers.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Regular hiking gear.
- Water: Lots of freshwater available.
- Weather hazards: Can be rainy/snowy during off season.
