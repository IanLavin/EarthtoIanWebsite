## Getting there
- Closest airport: Juan Santamaría International Airport
- Drive time: 3 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Nearly all activites and hikes start from La Fortuna.

## Time & season
- Best months: While it can rain year round, dry season is the most plesant. Typically Dec - April but late Febuary - March is most consistent
- Typical start time: Early morning to beat potential afternoon rain and heat
- Permit / reservation: Most hikes require some paid entry or activites are typically guided

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: If you have a car Tenorio National Park is only a 90 minute drive away and worth the effort.
- Good multi-day combo: Nearly all Costa Rica itineraries involve combining La Fortuna with a beach/costal stop, usually Manuel Antonio.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear, rainjacket, sunscreen, sunglasses, hat.
- Water: Most tap water in Costa Rica is safe to drink but buy bottled if worried.
- Weather hazards: Rain can occur year round but during dry season is more likely to occur in early evening/ overnight. Start early for best chance of clear weather.
