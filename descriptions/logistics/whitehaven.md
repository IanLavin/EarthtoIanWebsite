## Getting there
- Closest airport: Whitsundays or Airlie Beach
- Drive time: 1-2 hours by boat depending on port
- Trailhead / entry point: The viewpoint almost everyone goes to is Hill Inlet Overlook

## Time & season
- Best months: Australian winter. June - Sept
- Typical start time: Most boat tours leave in the morning 8am.
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Good multi-day combo: If you're in the whitsundays you're very likely here to also explore the Great Barrier Reef. While there are nearby sections you can visit the most impressive areas are a few hours north or south.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Swimsuit, Towel, Sunscreen
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Can get quite windy during some parts of the summer.
