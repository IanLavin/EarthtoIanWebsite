## Getting there
- Closest airport: Phoenix or Flagstaff
- Drive time: 4 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Nearly all paths to Supai and further into the canyon begin at Hualapai Hilltop

## Time & season
- Best months: April - May or Oct - Nov
- Typical start time: Early morning to avoid hiking in and out in the heat
- Permit / reservation: Strictly enforced permit system by the Havasupai tribe. Must be purchased far in advance and quite competitive to get

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Grand Canyon National park is both a short drive away, but can also be reached via hiking down to the confluence from the campground

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard backpacking gear, anticipate at least 3 nights and 4 days total
- Water: Available via springs at the campground
- Weather hazards: Heat is the most obvious and common factor to consider. However during the summer months June - Sept the canyon can be prone to flash flooding unexpectedly.
