## Getting there
- Closest airport: Atlanta or Birmingham
- Drive time: 2 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Cheaha is a state park 

## Time & season
- Best months: Fall Sept - Nov
- Typical start time: Park closes at sunset
- Permit / reservation: 10$ entrance fee per person

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Probably best paired with either a trip to Atlanta or Dismals canyon up north

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Normal hiking gear
- Water: Not required
- Weather hazards: Due to its larger prominence above surrounding landscape the summit can be prone to cloud whiteouts and thunderstorms.
