## Getting there
- Closest airport: Salt Lake City airport will very likely be your best option.
- Drive time: Around 4 hours from SLC.
- Trailhead / entry point: Arches contains a number of different trailheads but the most popular are Delicate Arch, Devils Garden, and Firey Furnace.

## Time & season
- Best months: Shoulder season provide best mix of plesant temperatures and clear weather. Think March-May and Sept-Oct
- Typical start time: Earlier the better, as parking can become difficult especially at popular trailheads like delicate arch.
- Permit / reservation: Standard national park pass.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: You will almost certainly be pairing this with canyonlands only an hour or two away.
- Good multi-day combo: For a truly epic expereince tag on some of the additonal nearby national parks such as capitol reef or Black Canyon of the Gunnison.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear.
- Water: Its the desert, bring more than you think you will need.
- Weather hazards: Heat will almost always be the biggest factor. Expect very litle shade and relentless sun exposure.
