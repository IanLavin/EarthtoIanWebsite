## Getting there
- Closest airport: Phoenix or Las Vegas are the two major choices
- Drive time: 3-4 hours depending on airport
- Trailhead / entry point: The vast majority of visitors will travel to the south rim and explore around the Bright Angel Trailhead.

## Time & season
- Best months: Suprisingly the canyon can get quite cold in winter. Best months are the shoulder ones, Mar - May or Sept - Nov
- Typical start time: Early morning to beat the crowds and heat
- Permit / reservation: Standard national park pass

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Flagstaff and Sedona are less than 2 hours away and provide additional majestic views.
- Good multi-day combo: One of the most popular road trip destinations in the world. You will very likely be visit as part of one of those.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear
- Water: Bring plenty
- Weather hazards: North rim closes due to snow during winter. Thunderstorms can also become a problem during monsoon season. Biggest factor by far however is usually the heat and the sun.
