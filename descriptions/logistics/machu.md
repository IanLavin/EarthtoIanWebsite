## Getting there
- Closest airport: Cusco
- Drive time: About four hours via a combination of bus and train, or 3-5 days via trekking.
- Trailhead / entry point: All tours to Machu Picchu will start with pickup in the town of Aguas Calientes.

## Time & season
- Best months: May - September. Clouds occur anytime throughout the year and obscur views but these months give greatest odds for clear weather.
- Typical start time: Shuttles depart Aguas Calients early morning before sunrise, typically 5AM.
- Permit / reservation: All walking tours require permits purchased in advance. Currently there are three one way circuit options with two being the most popular and three the least.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: You will pass through the town of Ollantaytambo which has some additional interesting ruins and areas to explore if time allows.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Normal walking gear.
- Water: Bring your own.
- Weather hazards: The area can often be shrouded in clouds or rainy so a light jacket is worth considering.
