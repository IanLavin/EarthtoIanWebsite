## Getting there
- Closest airport: Boise
- Drive time: 2 hours from Boise.
- Trailhead / entry point: Blue heart kayaking has their own launch point. Otherwise thousand springs can work.

## Time & season
- Best months: Summer months are best as winters are bitterly cold and windy.
- Typical start time: Early morning to avoid potential afternoon winds.
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Very likely you will be also stopping by thousand spring if you are out in this area.
- Good multi-day combo: Boise is the nearest major city but there are also other worthwile things to see nearby including Shoshone Falls or Craters of the Moon.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Kayak/Paddleboard, Swimsuit, Towel, Sunscreen
- Water: Bring your own.
- Weather hazards: Can be prone to strong winds making paddling difficult. Also quite cold and snowy in winter.
