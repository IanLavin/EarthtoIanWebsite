## Getting there
- Closest airport: Washington DC
- Drive time: 1 hour
- Trailhead / entry point: Nearly all trailheads connect back to the main skyline drive.

## Time & season
- Best months: Can be visited year round. Especially popular during peak fall colors. Oct - Nov
- Typical start time: Flexible
- Permit / reservation: Standard National Park pass

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Can be combined with other nearby parks or highpoints including New River Gorge, Spruce Knob, Backbone Mountain, or Mt Davis.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear
- Water: Bring your own
