## Getting there
- Closest airport: Albuquerque is the closest major airport
- Drive time: 5 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: All major sights located off of Highway 163 and Monument Valley road

## Time & season
- Best months: Shoulder months for best weather and temps. March - May or Sept - Nov
- Typical start time: Flexible but nice at sunrise/sunset
- Permit / reservation: Required if visiting portion controlled by Navajo Nation

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Four corners, Page AZ, Goosenecks state park are all popular and nearby.
- Good multi-day combo: Most likely you will be visiting as part of a southwest road trip.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear
- Water: Bring your own and plenty
- Weather hazards: Sun and heat exposure are the biggest danger typically.
