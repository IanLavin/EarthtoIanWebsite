## Getting there
- Closest airport: San Francisco, Oakland, or San Jose all work.
- Drive time: 1-3 hours depending on location and traffic.
- Trailhead / entry point: Rodeo Beach Trailhead.

## Time & season
- Best months: Summer provides the greatest chance of warm weather and clear skies. May - Sept
- Typical start time: Flexible
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Golden Gate national recreation areas is an underrated destination with lots of hidden beaches to be discovered.
- Good multi-day combo: Almost certainly you will be pairing this with a stay in the bay area and all of its associated sights.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Towel, Swimsuit, Sandals
- Water: Bring your own.
- Weather hazards: Water around the bay is known to be quite cold and probably unsuitable for swimming outside of hot days.
