## Getting there
- Closest airport: Columbia
- Drive time: 1 hour
- Trailhead / entry point: Harry Hampton Visitor Center 

## Time & season
- Best months: Spring or Fall
- Typical start time: Park is open 24/7
- Permit / reservation: No park pass required.

## What to pair it with
- Good multi-day combo: Best when paired with other things such as a trip to Atlanta or the southern Appalachians.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear. Bug spray is a requirement as mosquites can potentialy get quite overwhelming.
- Water: Bring your own.
- Weather hazards: Area is vulnerable to flooding and hurricanes during the summer and spring.
