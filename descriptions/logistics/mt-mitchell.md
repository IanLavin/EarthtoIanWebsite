## Getting there
- Closest airport: Charlotte or Asheville
- Drive time: 1-3 hours depending on airport
- Trailhead / entry point: Blue ridge parkway

## Time & season
- Best months: Fall Sept - Nov
- Typical start time: State park close times vary from 5-9pm.
- Permit / reservation: None required

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Best paired with other appalachian highpoints like Georgia, South Carolina, or Tennessee.
- Good multi-day combo: Great Smoky Mountains is ony two hours away.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear.
- Water: Bring your own.
- Weather hazards: The summit can get both heavy rain and snow and is frequently in the clouds. The area can also be hit by hurricanes and was closed due to damage from hurricane Helene for over a year.
