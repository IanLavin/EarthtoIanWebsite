## Getting there
- Closest airport: Portland or Redmond
- Drive time: 1-3 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Devils Lake/ South Sister Trailhead 

## Time & season
- Best months: When road to trailhead is clear but snow coverage is still decent, typically June - July
- Typical start time: Early, 4 - 5am
- Permit / reservation: South sister permit required, available on recreation.gov

## What to pair it with
- Good multi-day combo: Best paired with nearby waterfalls such as Tumalo or Salt Creek Falls.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear, microspikes/crampons depending on conditions, ice axe/trekking poles
- Water: Bring your own or be prepared to melt snow.
- Weather hazards: Like all Cascade volcanoes can be prone to bad weather. Start early and be prepared to bail if coulds begin to build on the summit.
