## Getting there
- Closest airport: Seattle Tacoma Airport
- Drive time: 4 hours to Rialto beach, 8 hours to complete a full loop.
- Trailhead / entry point: Three primary hub areas including Rialto Beach, Hoh Rainforest, and Sol Duc Falls.

## Time & season
- Best months: June - Sept
- Typical start time: During peak season parking lots often fill quickly so early starts are important.
- Permit / reservation: National Park pass

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Port Angeles is known for having good whale watching tours.
- Good multi-day combo: Usually paired with other Washington national parking like North Cascades or Rainier.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Normal hiking gear.
- Water: Plenty of water available.
- Weather hazards: One of the rainiest places in the United States. During peak season weather can be quite plesant but outside of that expect constant drizzle/rain.
