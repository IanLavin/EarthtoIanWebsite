## Getting there
- Closest airport: Hartford (BDL) or Albany (ALB)
- Drive time: ~1.5 hrs from BDL; ~1.5–2 hrs from ALB
- Trailhead / entry point: Commonly via the Mount Washington State Forest / South Slope trails area (MA side); CT highpoint is a shoulder, not the summit

## Time & season
- Best months: Late spring–fall; avoid muddy early spring if possible
- Typical start time: Morning to mid-day
- Permit / reservation: None typically, but check local parking/trail rules

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Berkshires, Great Barrington, Bash Bish Falls (nearby region)
- Good multi-day combo: CT + RI + MA (Greylock) in one loop

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Hiking shoes; ticks in warm months; trail map helpful
- Water: Bring at least 1L
- Weather hazards: Mud, heat/humidity in summer, slippery leaves in fall
