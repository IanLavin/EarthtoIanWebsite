## Getting there
- Closest airport: Sacramento is the biggest but Ashland or Redding are closer
- Drive time: 2-4 hours depending on airport.
- Trailhead / entry point: Burney Falls State Park

## Time & season
- Best months: Summer June - Sept
- Typical start time: As of 2026 a timed entry for parking is required to be reserved ahead of time.
- Permit / reservation: State park charges an entrance fee

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Shasta is very close by only an hour or two away.
- Good multi-day combo: Best paired with nearby Northern California attractions such as Redwoods, Shasta, Lassen, or Lake Tahoe.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear.
- Water: Bring your own.
- Weather hazards: Area can be at wildfire risk during late summer.
