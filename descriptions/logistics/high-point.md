## Getting there
- Closest airport: Newark (EWR) or NYC airports
- Drive time: ~1–1.5 hrs from EWR (traffic-dependent)
- Trailhead / entry point: High Point State Park—parking near the monument; short paved walk + optional trails

## Time & season
- Best months: Spring–fall; fall foliage is a highlight
- Typical start time: Morning on weekends to avoid crowds
- Permit / reservation: Park entry fee may apply (varies by season/residency)

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Delaware Water Gap, local lakes, scenic drives
- Good multi-day combo: NJ + CT + RI + MA on a Northeast highpoint swing

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Comfortable shoes; picnic gear if hanging out
- Water: Optional for short walks, bring more if hiking
- Weather hazards: Wind at the monument; icy paths in winter
