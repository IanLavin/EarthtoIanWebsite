## Getting there
- Closest airport: Phoenix
- Drive time: 2 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Fossil Creek Trailhead or Bob bear depending on which waterfall you want to visit.

## Time & season
- Best months: Spring - Fall is the best for warm swimming temps. April - Oct
- Typical start time: Early starts are required as you can be denied entrace by rangers for too late of arrival.
- Permit / reservation: Permits requried during peak season. Can be reserved online a month in advance.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: If coming from Pine side Tonto natural bridge is an excellent stop. Otherwise from highway 260 Sedona is the closest interesting destination.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear, swimsuit
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Heat can be an issue if not properly prepared. The trail has some shade but still expect high sun exposure.
