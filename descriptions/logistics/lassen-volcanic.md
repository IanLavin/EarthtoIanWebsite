## Getting there
- Closest airport: Sacramento, Redding, or Reno
- Drive time: 1-4 hours depending on airport
- Trailhead / entry point: Lassen peak trailhead

## Time & season
- Best months: Road typically only clear during summer. Mid June - Oct
- Typical start time: Flexible
- Permit / reservation: Standard national park pass

## What to pair it with
- Good multi-day combo: Best paired with other Northern California sights like Burney falls or Lake Tahoe.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear, microspike/crampons and ice axe depending on snow cover for Lassen Peak.
- Water: Bring your own.
- Weather hazards: The area can be prone to heavy snow in winter and summer storms on Lassen peak.
