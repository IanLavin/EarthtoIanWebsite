## Getting there
- Closest airport: El paso is the largest but Albuquerque also works as well
- Drive time: 1-3 hours depending on airport.
- Trailhead / entry point: Nearly all trails start from Alkali Trailhead.

## Time & season
- Best months: Shoulder months for clear weather and decent temps. April - June or Sept - Oct
- Typical start time: Flexible
- Permit / reservation: Standard national park pass.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Guadalupe Mountains and Carlsbad caverns national parks are the most obvious as they are only 2 hours away.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear. Sleds can be rented from the visitor center.
- Water: Bring your own
