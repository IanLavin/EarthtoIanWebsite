## Getting there
- Closest airport: Atlanta
- Drive time: 2 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Wagon Train trailhead is where both the shuttle and trail begin.

## Time & season
- Best months: Fall Sept - Nov
- Typical start time: The shuttle is only open until 5PM but the trail can be hiked 24/7

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Usually best paired with other nearby highpoints such as Tennessee and South Carolina.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear
- Water: Bring your own.
- Weather hazards: As part of the southern appalachians, the mountains quite often catch moisture from the gulf, and can be rainy fairly often.
