## Getting there
- Closest airport: Nashville or Louisville
- Drive time: 1-2 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Mammoth Cave Visitor Center

## Time & season
- Best months: Caves remain constant temp year round. Above ground the best time is summer/fall
- Typical start time: Guided cave tours usually require reservations ahead of time
- Permit / reservation: Standard national park pass

## What to pair it with
- Good multi-day combo: Best paired with a trip to experience Nashville

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear, heavy boots and pants if doing a caving tour
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Be prepared for the caves to be cold and damp
