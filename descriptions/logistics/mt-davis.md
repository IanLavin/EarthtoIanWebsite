## Getting there
- Closest airport: Pittsburgh (PIT)
- Drive time: ~1.5–2 hrs from PIT
- Trailhead / entry point: Mt Davis Natural Area—parking near the lookout tower; short walk/hike to the highpoint

## Time & season
- Best months: Spring–fall; winter possible but roads can be snowy
- Typical start time: Daylight hours
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Ohiopyle State Park (excellent), Fallingwater (further but iconic)
- Good multi-day combo: PA + MD + WV as a compact highpoint weekend

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Layers; shoes for short forest trails
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Snow/ice in winter; muddy trails after rain
