## Getting there
- Closest airport: Seattle Tacoma Airport
- Drive time: A little less than 3 hours to Diablo Lake
- Trailhead / entry point: Diablo Lake is the most popular destination. Many popular trailheads start at or are near it.

## Time & season
- Best months: June-Sept. Some roads can be closed due to snow in winter.
- Typical start time: For any lake activites early starts tend to be better as strong winds can develop throughout the day.
- Permit / reservation: National Park pass and some backcountry permits are required for camping.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Maple pass trailhead and Mt Baker are two additional popular and stunning nearby areas.
- Good multi-day combo: Can also be combined with other Washington parks such as Rainier.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Regular hiking gear.
- Water: Lots of freshwater available.
- Weather hazards: Can be prone to heavy rain and snow outside of peak season.
