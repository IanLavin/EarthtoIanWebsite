## Getting there
- Closest airport: You will probably choose Phoenix but Flagstaff has its own small airport as well.
- Drive time: 2 hours or so from Phoenix.
- Trailhead / entry point: The hike starts at the Snowbowl resort. You will probably be parking in their parking lot.

## Time & season
- Best months: May-Oct. Snow can sometimes linger into June depending on year. For the best expereince late September will have fields of golden aspens adding lovely fall colors. 
- Typical start time: Generally the earlier the better. If you can start between 7-9AM you should be able to finish before sunset.
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: There are a series of lava tubes you can explore nearby called Lava River Cave.
- Good multi-day combo: Quite a few potential options for pairing with nearby. The most obvious is the Grand Canyon only an hour and a half away, but Sedona and Petrified forests are strong alternitives as well.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Regular hiking gear.
- Water: None on mountain, bring your own.
- Weather hazards: Summer thunderstorms should be taken seriously. People can and have been struck by lighting while on the summit ridge. Be aware of the forecast and start early.
