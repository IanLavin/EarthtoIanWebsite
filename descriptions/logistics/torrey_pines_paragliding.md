## Getting there
- Closest airport: San Diego
- Drive time: 45 mins
- Trailhead / entry point: Torrey Pines Gliderport

## Time & season
- Best months: Accessible year round
- Typical start time: Depends upon winds but usually late morning through afternoon.
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: La Jolla and Torrey Pines state beach are both nice to visit.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Paragliding equipment required
