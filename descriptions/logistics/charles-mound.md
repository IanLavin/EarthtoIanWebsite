## Getting there
- Closest airport: Chicago ORD or MDW
- Drive time: 3-4 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Private property, parking only allowed in certain areas.

## Time & season
- Best months: Only open to the public on select dates. Typically presidents day weekend as well as the first full weekends of June, July, August, and September.

## What to pair it with
- Good multi-day combo: Since you will most likely be visitng here during the summer is is best paired with Chicago and the great lakes.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Regular hiking gear.
- Water: Bring your own.
- Weather hazards: Snow can be a concern over presidents day weekend.
