## Getting there
- Closest airport: Charlotte or Greenville
- Drive time: 1-3 hours depending on airport
- Trailhead / entry point: There is a small dedicated trailhead near the summit

## Time & season
- Best months: Fall Sept - Nov
- Typical start time: Open 24/7
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Whitewater falls is only 45 mins away and is the tallest waterfall east of the Mississippi
- Good multi-day combo: Usually best combined with either other nearby appalachian highpoints or Congaree national park.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear
- Water: Bring/buy your own
- Weather hazards: Can be prone to rain and clouds in the spring and summer, also potential for hurricane damage during hurricane season.
