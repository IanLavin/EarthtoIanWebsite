## Time & season
- Best months: Australian winter is actually the best time to visit due to clear weather and waters. Typically May - Oct
- Typical start time: Most boat tours depart early in the morning around 8AM.
- Permit / reservation: Usually covered as part of boat tours.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: The Daintree rainforest stretches along most of the coast near the reef and contains numerous sights to see.
- Good multi-day combo: Outside of Daintree rainfalls, some interesting nearby locations include the Whitsundays, Wallaman Falls, Australias highest falls, and the Atherton Tablelands.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Swimsuit, Snorkle, Scuba Gear, Sunscreen, etc
- Water: Usually available through boat tours.
- Weather hazards: During the rainy season conditions can become unsuitable for driving to ports and result in poor water clarity.
