## Getting there
- Closest airport: San Diego or Los Angeles are the closest major airports.
- Drive time: 2-4 hours depending on airport.
- Trailhead / entry point: Either DeAnza Spring or Mortero Palms

## Time & season
- Best months: Temperature is a serious concern so winter or spring are the most plesant. Jan - April
- Typical start time: Early morning to avoid the heat and sun.
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Three sisters falls or Imperial sand dunes are only a few hours away and provide unique landscapes.
- Good multi-day combo: Most likely you will be combining this with spending some time in San Diego as it is the closest major city.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear.
- Water: Bring more than you think you'll need.
- Weather hazards: There is almost no shade along this path. Prepare for heat and high sun exposure.
