## Getting there
- Closest airport: Phoenix, San Diego, or Los Angeles
- Drive time: 2-3 hours depending on airport
- Trailhead / entry point: Pulloffs from either Interstate 8 or Highway 78

## Time & season
- Best months: Dunes can get quite hot during the summer months. Jan - April or Oct - Dec
- Typical start time: Flexible but nice at sunset especially.
- Permit / reservation: None if passing through, required if camping.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Joshua Tree is only a few hours away.
- Good multi-day combo: Almost always visited when driving between cities such as Phoenix and Las Vegas or San Diego.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard walking gear.
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Heat and sun are big factors during the summer months.
