## Getting there
- Closest airport: Denver or Albuquerque
- Drive time: 5-6 hours depending on airport
- Trailhead / entry point: South Mineral Campground

## Time & season
- Best months: Summer - Fall for melted lakes and clear trails. July - Sept
- Typical start time: Early morning 
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Telluride or Ouray are good nearby options.
- Good multi-day combo: Best done as part of a roadtrip around southwestern Colorado.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear
- Water: Bring your own or filter
- Weather hazards: Summer storms are a concern like in most of the Rocky Mountains.
