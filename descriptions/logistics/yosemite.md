## Getting there
- Closest airport: Merced, Sacramento, or Fresno
- Drive time: 2-4 hours depending on airport
- Trailhead / entry point: Most major hikes start from portal rd

## Time & season
- Best months: May - Oct
- Typical start time: Early to beat the massive crowds
- Permit / reservation: Standard national park pass

## What to pair it with
- Good multi-day combo: If you have time Kings Canyon/Sequoia is only a few hours drive away.  

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Prone to heavy snow during winter months and frequent afternoon stomrs during the summer.
