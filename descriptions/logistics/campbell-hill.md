## Getting there
- Closest airport: Dayton (DAY) or Columbus (CMH)
- Drive time: ~20–30 min from DAY; ~1.5 hrs from CMH
- Trailhead / entry point: Wright-Patterson AFB area (near the official marker/park spot)

## Time & season
- Best months: Spring–Fall (pleasant walking weather)
- Typical start time: Daytime; check access hours if within/adjacent to base-managed areas
- Permit / reservation: No permit, but access rules can change—confirm current public access before arriving

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: National Museum of the U.S. Air Force (huge), Dayton breweries/food
- Good multi-day combo: Hoosier Hill (IN) + Michigan/Wisconsin/Illinois routing

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Normal shoes; bring ID just in case (depending on access point)
- Water: Optional
- Weather hazards: Minimal—heat/cold depending on season
