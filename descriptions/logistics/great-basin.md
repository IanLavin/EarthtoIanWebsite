## Getting there
- Closest airport: Salt Lake City or Las Vegas
- Drive time: 4 hours 
- Trailhead / entry point: Bristlecone, Alpine Lakes trailhead

## Time & season
- Best months: Peak and roads are often snow covered through spring. Summer is the best weather, June - Sept
- Typical start time: Early morning to avoid afternoon storms and winds
- Permit / reservation: Standard national park pass.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: This park is truly in the middle of nowhere Nevada, the most popular thing around it is Highway 50 nicknamed "The Loneliest Road in America".

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard Hiking Gear
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Prone to storms and wind, like most tall peaks.
