## Getting there
- Closest airport: Phoenix
- Drive time: 2 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Salome Wilderness Trailhead

## Time & season
- Best months: If you want to experience the famous "waterslide" you need to go when snowmelt is between safe and unsafe level. This typically occurs during May or occasionally early June.
- Typical start time: Early morning to avoid heat.
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: You'll be passing by Roosevelt Lake.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Hiking gear, Swimsuit, Sunscreen, Harness and Gear if planning to rappel final jump.
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: As a slot canyon always vulnerable to flash flooding during monsoon season. Should also carefully monitor reports to check for safe/unsafe water flow levels.
