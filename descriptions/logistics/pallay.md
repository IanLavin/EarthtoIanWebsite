## Getting there
- Closest airport: Cusco
- Drive time: 4 hours each way from cusco.
- Trailhead / entry point: Typically accessed via a small dirt road coming down from the north side off of highway 3s.

## Time & season
- Best months: May - Sept
- Typical start time: Most day tours leave early around 4-5 AM.
- Permit / reservation: You are probably best off booking with a tour company to visit as the final few miles of road to the location are extremely rough and prone to getting cars stuck if you don't know what you are doing.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Almost always done as a day trip, though if you are feeling ambitious Lake Titicaca is only a few additional hours away.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Regular Hiking Gear
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Typical high altitude weather. Expect cold, wind, and potentially clouds obscuring your view.
