## Getting there
- Closest airport: Washington Dulles (IAD) or Charlottesville (CHO) (regional options vary)
- Drive time: ~2.5–3.5 hrs from IAD; ~2.5–3.5 hrs from CHO
- Trailhead / entry point: Spruce Knob summit parking area; short paved/boardwalk path to viewpoint + marker

## Time & season
- Best months: Late spring–fall (roads easier), fall foliage is excellent
- Typical start time: Morning or sunset for views; weekday if you want it quieter
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Dolly Sods Wilderness, Seneca Rocks, Blackwater Falls (further)
- Good multi-day combo: WV + VA + PA highpoints in one trip

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Layers—it’s windy/cool even in summer; good shoes if hiking nearby trails
- Water: Bring your own if doing longer hikes (Dolly Sods, etc.)
- Weather hazards: Fog, high winds, sudden storms; snow/ice can linger
