## Getting there
- Closest airport: Albuquerque
- Drive time: 2-3 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Williams Lake trailhead.

## Time & season
- Best months: Summer - Fall when the snow melts. May - Sept
- Typical start time: Early morning to avoid storms
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Taos and Santa Fe are nice stops nearby.
- Good multi-day combo: If you have some extra time Great Sand Dunes national park is only about 2 hours away.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear, Crampons/Ice Axe if off season
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Summer storms are common, try to be off peak before afternoon.
