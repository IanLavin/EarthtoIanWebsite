## Getting there
- Closest airport: Pittsburgh (PIT) or Washington Dulles (IAD)
- Drive time: ~2–2.5 hrs from PIT; ~2.5–3.5 hrs from IAD
- Trailhead / entry point: Near Hoye-Crest (MD highpoint); short hike from the closest parking pull-off/trail access

## Time & season
- Best months: Late spring–fall; fall colors are great
- Typical start time: Mid-morning to afternoon
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Deep Creek Lake, Swallow Falls State Park
- Good multi-day combo: MD + WV (Spruce Knob) + PA (Mt Davis)

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Short hike but can be muddy; bug spray in warm months
- Water: Bring a bottle
- Weather hazards: Mud, slick leaves in fall; snow/ice in winter
