## Getting there
- Closest airport: Denver Airport
- Drive time: 2 hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Can drive to summit via Pikes Peak Highway

## Time & season
- Best months: May - Sept
- Typical start time: Variable
- Permit / reservation: Timed permits are required to be purchased beforehand if driving your own car to the summit.

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Garden of the gods is along the way and an extremely popular destination.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Regular walking gear
- Water: Available
- Weather hazards: The summit is prone to storms. There is a large lodge at the summit that can provide shelter however.
