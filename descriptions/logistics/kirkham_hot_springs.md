## Getting there
- Closest airport: Boise
- Drive time: 2 Hours
- Trailhead / entry point: Kirkham Hot Springs

## Time & season
- Best months: Year round but can be especially cozy during the winter surrounded by snow.
- Typical start time: Flexible
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Stanley/Sawtooths are only a little over an hour away and provide lovely views.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Swimsuit, Towel, Walking Gear
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Heavy snow can lead to road closures.
