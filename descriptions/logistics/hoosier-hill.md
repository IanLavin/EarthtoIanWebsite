## Getting there
- Closest airport: Indianapolis (IND) or Dayton (DAY)
- Drive time: ~1.5–2 hrs from IND; ~2 hrs from DAY
- Trailhead / entry point: Small parking area along a rural road; short signed walk to the highpoint marker

## Time & season
- Best months: Year-round (best comfort in spring/fall)
- Typical start time: Anytime daylight—super quick stop
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Dayton/Indy city stops, Richmond IN, local parks
- Good multi-day combo: Combine with Campbell Hill (OH) + other Midwest highpoints (IL/WI/MI if routing)

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Normal shoes; stay on the marked path
- Water: Not necessary for the short walk
- Weather hazards: Mud after rain; cold/wind in winter
