## Getting there
- Closest airport: Fresno or Bakersfield
- Drive time: 2-3 hours depending on airport
- Trailhead / entry point: Most sights are located off of highway 180.

## Time & season
- Best months: May - Oct
- Typical start time: Flexible
- Permit / reservation: Standard national park pass

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Almost always paired with nearby Sequoia
- Good multi-day combo: Also goes well with Yosemite national park a few hours drive north.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear.
- Water: Bring your own
- Weather hazards: Prone to roads closing and heavy snow in winter, afternoon thunderstorms in summer.
