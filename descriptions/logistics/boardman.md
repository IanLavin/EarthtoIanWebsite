## Getting there
- Closest airport: Ashland is closest but Sacramento or Portland provide more connections.
- Drive time: 2-6 hours depending on airport.
- Trailhead / entry point: Multiple pull offs along road.

## Time & season
- Best months: Summer is the driest and best for coastal views. June - Sept
- Typical start time: Flexible
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Good multi-day combo: Best paired with other nearby national parks such as Lassen Volcanic, Crater Lake, or Redwoods. 

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear.
- Water: Bring your own.
- Weather hazards: Can be muddy/rainy during off season. Also be prepared for cool damp conditions at night.
