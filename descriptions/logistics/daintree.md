## Getting there
- Closest airport: Cairns
- Drive time: 2:30 hours from Cairns to Cape Tribulation. Ferry Crossing required.
- Trailhead / entry point: Many trails to explore throughout the region. Cape tribulation is one of the most popular.

## Time & season
- Best months: Dry season during Australian winter May - Sept
- Typical start time: Flexible
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Best paired with nearby daintree experiences such as river boat crocodile cruises.
- Good multi-day combo: If you're in this region you're almost certainly here to see the Great Barrier Reef as well.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear.
- Water: Bring your own.
- Weather hazards: Rain is a problem during the off season as roads can flood and trails close.
