## Getting there
- Closest airport: Sedona has an airport but it's likely much cheaper to fly into Phoenix and drive
- Drive time: 2 hours from PHX
- Trailhead / entry point: Popular trailheads include Bell Rock, Cathedral Rock, Soldiers Pass, Devils Bridge, and Boynton Canyon.

## Time & season
- Best months: Shoulder months are the best combo of weather and temps. Feb - May and Sept - Nov
- Typical start time: Flexible
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Easy to combine with a trip up to Flagstaff.
- Good multi-day combo: The Grand Canyon is the biggest draw nearby and is only about 2 hours away.

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Standard hiking gear.
- Water: Bring your own and plenty
- Weather hazards: Heat and sun exposure will typically be the biggest concerns.
