## Getting there
- Closest airport: Denver (DEN) (common hub for the region)
- Drive time: Long rural drives; plan fuel stops
- Trailhead / entry point: Near the NE/WY/CO tri-state area—short walk to the highpoint marker

## Time & season
- Best months: Spring/fall (cooler, less intense storms)
- Typical start time: Daytime; sunsets can be great but watch road conditions
- Permit / reservation: None

## What to pair it with
- Nearby stops: Scotts Bluff National Monument, Chimney Rock (depending on route)
- Good multi-day combo: Panorama Point + Mount Sunflower + Black Mesa loop

## Safety & prep
- Gear notes: Normal shoes; bring snacks
- Water: Bring water—very remote
- Weather hazards: Wind and thunderstorms; winter snow/drifts possible
