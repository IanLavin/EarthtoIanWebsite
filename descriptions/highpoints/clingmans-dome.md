Clingmans Dome is the highest point in Tennessee and the tallest peak in Great Smoky Mountains National Park, just shy of Mount Mitchell for the eastern US crown. A steep half-mile paved trail leads to a concrete observation tower with a distinctive spiraling ramp, offering panoramic views across a sea of blue-hazed ridgelines. The summit is one of the most visited spots in the most visited national park in the country, and on clear days the views stretch well over 100 miles.
