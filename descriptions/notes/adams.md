- Mt. Adams is one of the largest of the Cascade volcanoes in terms of volume. second only to Mt. Shasta.
- The climb can be done as either one long day of 6200ft of elevation gain, or as a two day trip broken up by camping at Lunch Counter.
- While the mountain is glaciated, the south climb is completely crevasse free, meaning it can be climbed solo without a rope team. 
- The road to cold springs campground requires four wheel drive and can be quite steep at points.
- Despite the snow and cold, sunburn is a real concern on the mountain. Glasses with UV protection are especially important for preventing snow blindness.
