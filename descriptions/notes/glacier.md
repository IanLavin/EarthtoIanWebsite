- The parks contains a unique peak known as triple divide peak. Water that falls on the mountain ends up in three different destinations, the Pacific Ocean, Atlantic Ocean, and Hudson Bay.
- When the park was established in 1910 it had 150 named glaciers. Today that number is down to only 25.
- The park shares a boundary with a Canadian national park, Waterton National Park.
- The Going the Sun Route is famously featured in the opening of the movie the Shining.
