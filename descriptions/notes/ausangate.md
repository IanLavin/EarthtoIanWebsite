## Notes
- A hidden gem around Cusco, this peak is actually the tallest in the region at 20,945ft
- Despite the name seven sacred lakes, there may be more or less present depending on recent rain and snowfalls totals.
- This area is a traditional alpaca and llama free range area, and you will often pass by groups foraging on the short grasses.
- Due to past volcanic activity there are also the occascional hot springs scattered throughout the area.
