- Permits for Havasupai are extremly competitive to get, often selling out withing hours or even minutes of being released. Typically there is a yearly sale on Febuary 1st however presales can also happen and should always be utilized if possible.
- This is one of the most expensive backpacing permits in the US due to high demand. As of 2026 permits can run upwards of $450.
- The bright blue color of the water comes from the natural springs supplying Havasu Creek, they are filled with calcium carbonate and limestone which reflect light back strongly in the blue range.
- This high mineral concentration also means the path of the stream is constantly changing, with new banks forming and the pool at the base of the falls nearly always in flux.
